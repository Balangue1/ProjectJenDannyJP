EAT!     DRINK!    PLAY!

Welcome to Eat Drink Play. We're glad you stopped by. Ever have one of those days where nothing you do ever pleases the boss? You know what it's like to spend your entire day having to make the tough calls and the important decisions. Now you come home, and there's still more thinking to do. You have to figure out what you want to eat, maybe whether you'll have that cocktail, and how you are just going to get your poor frazzled brain to chill! Well you can relax, we've got you covered!

Below we will help you figure out something to eat, and perhaps a drink to go with it. And once you're all set on dining and a beverage, we'll set you up with a game you can play so you can give your tired brain a break!

Please drink responsibly. And if you do drink, please don't drive!
