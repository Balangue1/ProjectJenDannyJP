How to Play Card Matching Game

  1. Select your card suit by clicking on the Spade, Heart, Diamond or Club icon.
  2. Next select your card face by clicking on the Ace, King, Queen, Jack or one of the number cards. This will load your first card selection.
  3. Repeat the above two steps until you have four face-down cards on your screen.
  4. To see if you have won, now click the Draw the Card button to have the computer generate four cards to match against yours.
  5. If the computer card on the bottom row matches your card sitting above it you will win.
  6. If there is no match you will receive a Sorry! Try again! message next to the card suit row.
  7. To play again, simply click the Start the Game button to reset the game and select another four cards following the above instructions.
